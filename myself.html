MySelf:

I am a natural light photographer with a unique artistic style and a love for candid captures! My personal goal is to have some fun making something fabulous!

I specialize in portraits for Glamour, Fashion, Families, Couples, Maternity sessions and styled model and senior photos.
I am willing in wedding, boudoir, and studio work, as well, so don’t be afraid to ask for something outside of the box!

It was when an old hobby became a profession. Photography! It was only in Paris that I took my 1st model shoot and decided to plunge into this wonderful new world – doing workshops, studying photo processing, technicians and investing in equipment's that showcase the my style. The acceptance and encouragement from people around me and from other professionals make me feel that I am on the right path.

I'm Located in Pittsburgh, PA and serving surrounding areas. I am also willing to travel!

I am always interested in working with new models that are looking to add something different to their portfolios and I will offer free shoot's for the model's who are about to build their portfolios.
I have worked with a number of wonderful models and look forward to working with fresh talent!
